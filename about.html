History

Calvada Surveying is a Disabled Veteran and Minority Owned land surveying firm founded in 1989 by Armando DuPont, PLS, and Edward Schenet, PLS. Our company headquarters are located in Corona, California. The year 1996 marked the start of an intense growth period for the firm and since that time we have achieved steady growth and established branch offices in Northern California, Colorado, Arizona, and Nevada. Our strategically located offices enable the firm to fully serve the project needs of our clients in California, Colorado, Wyoming, New Mexico, Arizona, Nevada, Utah, and Texas.

Our Mission

To utilize our company’s years of experience and expertise to set the standard for excellence in the industry by providing the highest quality and most efficient land surveying services. Success will be measured by our ability to earn the trust of each of our clients and maintain our reputation for superior quality within the industry.

Our Vision

Become one of the leading land surveying firms in the Western United States, enabling the growth and prosperity of the company so that we may perpetually serve the needs of our clients and communities, as well as provide for the future of our employees, their families, and generations to come.

What We Do

Calvada Surveying is a leading provider of professional land surveying services throughout the Western United States. Our expertise lies within the engineering, development, real estate, environmental, and telecommunication industries, serving both the private and public sectors.

We are committed to implementing innovative services and are proud to offer the latest in land surveying technology including High Definition Surveying (HDS), also known as 3D laser scanning. This powerful technology allows us to provide accurate 3D drawings more quickly and saves our clients countless hours and cost. We also now offer drone surveying which can be used on certain projects to limit the amount of time our crews spend in the field.
