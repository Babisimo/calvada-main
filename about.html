About Calvada Surveying

Founded in 1989, Calvada Surveying, Inc. is a distinguished Disabled Veteran and Minority Owned land surveying firm headquartered in Corona, California. Pioneered by industry experts Armando DuPont and Edward Schenet, our firm has maintained a strong presence throughout the Western United States, focusing on serving clients in Southern and Northern California, Washington, Oregon, and Arizona. Explore our locations to learn more.

Our Mission

Calvada Surveying employs decades of expertise and cutting-edge surveying techniques to define industry standards. We are dedicated to delivering top-tier surveying services, building trust with our team, and maintaining our longstanding reputation for excellence. Discover our comprehensive services.

Our Vision

Our aim is to continue leading as a premier land surveying firm in the Western US, supporting the growth and success of our clients' projects and the communities we serve, while securing the future for our employees and their families.
