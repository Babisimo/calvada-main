ALTA/NSPS Land Title Surveys

The information provided on ALTA/NSPS land title surveys is used to assist title companies, lenders, and borrowers identify potential risks when evaluating properties for purchase. These surveys include the property boundaries, easements, existing improvements (buildings, walls, trails, roads, etc.), flood zone, zoning, parking summary, encumbrances, and potential encroachments on the subject property.

For the survey of real property to be acceptable to a title insurance company for the purpose of insuring title to said real property free and clear of survey matters (except those matters disclosed by the survey and indicated on the plat or map), certain specific and pertinent information must be presented for the distinct and clear understanding between the insured, the client (if different from the insured), the title insurance company (insurer), the lender, and the surveyor professionally responsible for the survey.

In order to meet such needs, clients, insurers, insureds, and lenders rely on Calvada to conduct surveys and prepare associated plats or maps that are of a professional quality and appropriately uniform, complete, and accurate.
