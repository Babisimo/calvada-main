Welcome to Calvada Surveying, Inc.! Calvada Surveying is a Veteran and Minority-Owned Oregon surveying firm/company providing complete land surveying services and technology including topographic mapping, boundary surveys, GPS land Surveys, ALTA/NSPS, construction staking, Laser Scanning High, Definition Survey, preparation of tract , Geodetic survey and parcel maps, environmental surveys and earth work calculation and quantities, and land title surveys in accordance with the American Land Title Association and the American Congress on Surveying and Mapping. Our professional land surveyors provide land surveying services in Oregon, Arizona, California, Colorado, Nevada, Utah, Washington and Wyoming.

Calvada Surveying is headquartered in Corona, California, with a regional office in Seattle, Washington. This office handles the Oregon surveying projects throughout the State of Washington. Calvada Surveying has the technical surveying expertise with its knowledgeable staff of Professional Land Surveyors (PLS), Project Managers, and Survey Technicians. Calvada Surveying has eleven (11) field survey crews that can handle any type of survey project within Oregon.

Our surveyors are specialized individuals, whose education and experience have been rigorously examined and who have been licensed by the state authority to practice land surveying. Our surveyors provide timely and expert land surveying services to architects, designers, engineers and builders.
