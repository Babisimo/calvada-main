Welcome to Calvada Surveying, Inc.! Calvada Surveying is a Veteran and Minority-Owned land surveying firm providing complete land surveying and mapping services including topographic mapping, boundary surveying, construction staking, preparation of tract and parcel maps, and earth work calculation and quantities, and land title surveys in accordance with the American Land Title Association and the American Congress on Surveying and Mapping. We are professional land surveyors providing surveying services in Colorado, Arizona, California, Nevada, Utah and Wyoming.

Calvada Surveying is headquartered in Corona, California, with a regional office in Denver, Colorado. This office handles the surveying projects throughout the State of Colorado. Calvada Surveying has the technical surveying expertise with its very knowledgeable staff of Professional Land Surveyors (PLS), Project Managers, and Survey Technicians. Calvada Surveying has eleven (11) field survey crews that can handle any type of survey project within Colorado, New Mexico, Utah and Wyoming.
